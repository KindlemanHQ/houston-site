Houston. Admin utility for your all your Rails Apps.

Houston provides a robust admin utility for your data and content. No DSL. No weird code. Just your data, easily and securely managed.

Features

Houston comes with a rich feature set that adapts to you your needs and your apps data.

Model Management

Easily scaffold and then adapt admin areas for your models.

Snippet Management

A simple way to manage all the little bits of text around your site.

User Management

Manage your users and team accounts from within Houston. Houston includes advanced features to help you support your users.

Menu Management

Install Houston::MenuManagement to enable elegant, intuitive management of all your site menus.

Media Library

Enable the Media Library feature to provide a feature rich media management area and file picker

RichText Areas

Houston comes with a preinstalled Markdown editor to allow your admin users to add rich, engaging and informative content.

Block Based Pages

Enable Houston pages to build content pages with flexible content blocks.

Useful Field Types

Houston comes with a solid set of field types. It's also just rails, so you it couldn't be simpler to add more.
